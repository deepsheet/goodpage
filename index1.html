Frequently Asked Questions

What is GoodPage?

GoodPage is an AI-powered text beautification tool that automatically converts plain text into beautiful HTML webpages. It uses intelligent layout algorithms to add gradients, card layouts, emojis, and other visual elements to make your text more engaging.

Is GoodPage free to use?

Yes, completely free! GoodPage currently offers a fully free service with no registration required. Generated webpages receive permanent links that can be shared and accessed anytime.

Can the generated webpages work offline?

Yes! All styles are embedded inline in the HTML files without relying on external resources. You can download the generated HTML file and it will display perfectly even without an internet connection.

What languages are supported?

Currently supports Chinese and English. You can switch languages in the top-right corner of the page, and the system will automatically adapt the interface text accordingly.

How do I share my beautified article?

After beautification, the system generates a unique URL link. Click "Copy Link" and share it on WeChat, Weibo, Moments, or other social platforms. Anyone can view your beautiful article through this link.
